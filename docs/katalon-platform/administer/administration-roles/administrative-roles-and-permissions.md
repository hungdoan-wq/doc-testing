This document discusses the different levels of administration in Katalon TestOps.

<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p"><span className="ph">Katalon TestOps</span> has 3 (three) management levels:</p> 
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p"><ul className="ul"><li className="li">Account</li><li className="li">Organization</li><li className="li">Project</li></ul></div>
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">The Account level is the highest level, followed by the Organization level, then the Project level.</p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">The Account level is in charge of subscription and billing management functions. One Account is associated with one Organization.</p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">At the Organization level, members can collaborate across several projects. One Organization can own  one or multiple Projects.</p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">The Project level facilitates the management of a project and its members.</p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">​<span className="ph">Katalon TestOps</span>​ organizes users into 4 (four) specific roles:</p> 
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p"><ul className="ul"><li className="li"><strong className="ph b">Owner:</strong> The Owner has the highest authority that has full permissions. This is the default role for the user who creates an Account, Organization, or Project.</li><li className="li"><strong className="ph b">Administrator:</strong> The Administrator has the next level of permissions, they are in charge of administrative functions. This role is granted by the Owner.</li><li className="li"><strong className="ph b">Billing Manager:</strong> The Billing Manager is responsible for managing billing for Katalon Platform subscriptions. This role is granted by the Owner or Admins. A Billing Manager is under Account level  and cannot be added to the Project.</li><li className="li"><strong className="ph b">Member:</strong> Members have the lowest level of permissions. This role is automatically assigned to any person accepting the invitation to join an Organization or Project. They can view and run tests, but they do not have any added administrative functions by default. They can be assigned different roles to expand their privileges.</li></ul></div>
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="note note note_note"><span className="note__title">Note:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li">For organizations created before December 20th 2023, Katalon still maintains the old structure which has 4 management levels: Account, Organization, Team, and Project. The Account level is the highest, followed by the Organization, Team, and then  Project levels.</li></ul></div>
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">For a more specific breakdown of what each role's permissions are, refer to:</p> 
<ul xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p"><a className="xref" href="#concept-8930">Roles and permissions at Account and Organization level</a></p></li><li className="li"><p className="p"><a className="xref" href="#topic-5961">Roles and permissions at the Project level</a></p></li><li className="li"><p className="p"><a className="xref" href="#topic-r1wm5luw">Roles and permissions at the Team level</a> (For organizations created before December 20<sup className="ph sup">th</sup>, 2023)</p></li></ul>
