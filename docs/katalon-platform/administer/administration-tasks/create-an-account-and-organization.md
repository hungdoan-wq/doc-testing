## <a id="task-6742" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Create an Account

<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Sign in to <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://testops.katalon.io/login" target="_blank">Katalon TestOps</a>. ### <a id="concept-8037" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Manage an Account

<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="shortdesc">The following guide shows you how to manage your Account information.</p> 
<h4 xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="title topictitle4 anchor_top_offset" id="task-505">View and update Account information</h4> 
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context"><div className="note important note_important"><span className="note__title">Important:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li">You must be the Owner or Admin of your Account.</li></ul></div><div className="note note note_note"><span className="note__title">Note:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">The Billing Manager of an Account can only view the Account information.</p></li></ul></div><p className="p">To view and update your Account's name, follow these steps:</p></section> 
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Log in to <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://my.katalon.com/" target="_blank">TestOps Admin</a> and select your Account.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">On the Account admin page, select the <span className="ph uicontrol">Account Settings</span> section on the sidebar.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><p className="p">In <span className="ph uicontrol">Account Settings</span>, you can find the following information:</p><ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">The number of Members, Organizations, and Projects that belong to your Account</p></li><li className="li"><p className="p">The Account ID</p></li></ul><img className="image" width={800} src={useBaseUrl("/912a75d0-9ef1-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/KT-View_Account_Settings.png")} alt="View Account Settings" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">You can click <span className="ph uicontrol">Edit Name</span> to change your Account's name.</span></li></ol> 
<h4 xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="title topictitle4 anchor_top_offset" id="task-qkx3kgmh">Delete an Account</h4> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="shortdesc">This document will guide you on the steps necessary to remove your Account permanently from the Katalon Platform. </p> 
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context"><div className="note important note_important"><span className="note__title">Important:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">You must be the Owner of your Account.</p></li></ul></div><div className="note warning note_warning"><span className="note__title">Warning:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">This action will permanently delete your Account including your Organization, test plans, licenses, and remove all Projects associations. Proceed with caution as this cannot be undone.</p></li></ul></div><p className="p">Follow these steps to delete an Account.</p></section> 
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">On the Account admin page, select <span className="ph uicontrol">Account Settings</span> section found in the bottom of the sidebar.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><p className="p">The <span className="ph uicontrol">Account Settings</span> page appears.</p></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Navigate to the <span className="ph uicontrol">Danger zone</span> section, then select the <span className="ph uicontrol">Delete Account</span> button. </span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={800} src={useBaseUrl("/9134af00-9ef1-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/KT-Account_Settings.png")} alt="Account settings in Katalon TestOps." /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">A pop-up appears to confirm your decision by typing your Account's name in. Input your Account's name and press <span className="ph uicontrol">Delete this account</span>. </span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={500} src={useBaseUrl("/91203ca0-9ef1-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/KT-Delete_your_account.png")} /><p className="p">This will permanently delete your Account including your Organization, test plans, licenses, and remove all Projects associations.</p></div></li></ol> 
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section result">You are redirected back to the homepage. Note that it may take some time before your account is completely removed. </section>
