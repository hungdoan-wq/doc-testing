## <a id="task-2158" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Accept invitation to join an Organization

<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="shortdesc"> </p> 
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context"><p className="p">If the email domain of the invited users is a public one (e.g., <code className="ph codeph">@gmail.com</code>) or does not match the email domain of either the Account/Organization Owner or the inviter, the invited users will receive an invitation email to join an Organization.</p><p className="p">To accept the invitation to join an Organization, follow these steps:</p></section>
