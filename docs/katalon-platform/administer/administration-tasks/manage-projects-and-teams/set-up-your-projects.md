<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">A Project is created to organize and manage the members involved in the same Projects. Only members can view and access their projects.</p> 

## Requirement

<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">You must be the Owner or Admin of your Project or Organization. Refer to the following topic for further information about roles and permissions: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/administration-roles/administrative-roles-and-permissions">Administrative Roles and Permissions</a>.</p> 

## <a id="task-8hd39a38" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Create a Project

<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context"><p className="p">To create a Project, follow these steps:</p></section> 
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Sign in to <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://my.katalon.com/" target="_blank">TestOps Admin</a>.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">On the Account admin page, select your Organization.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={700} src={useBaseUrl("/505a4ce0-bf78-11ee-ac6d-0242c7a41fd4/KT-Acount-page.png")} alt="Account page" /></div><div className="itemgroup stepresult">You are redirected to the <span className="ph uicontrol">Project Management</span> page of that Organization.</div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Select <span className="ph uicontrol">Create New Project</span> in the upper right corner.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={700} src={useBaseUrl("/3f9eb790-9ef0-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/KT-Create_a_Project.png")} alt="Project Management page" /></div><div className="itemgroup stepresult"> <p className="p">The <span className="ph uicontrol">Create A Project</span> dialog pops up.</p><img className="image" width={500} src={useBaseUrl("/3f9345e0-9ef0-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/KT-Create_a_Project_dialog.png")} alt="Create a project dialog" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Enter your desired Project name  and click <span className="ph uicontrol">Create</span>.</span></li></ol> 
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section result">You are now the Owner of your Project and   have full permissions of an Owner. For more information about roles and permissions, refer to: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/administration-roles/administrative-roles-and-permissions#topic-5961">Roles and permissions at the Project level</a>.</section> 

### <a id="task-zd2n49av" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Add users to a Project

<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context"><p className="p">You can invite new users to your Project and they will automatically be added to your Organization right after.</p><p className="p">To add users to your project, follow these steps:</p></section> 
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">On the Account admin page, select your Organization.</span><div className="itemgroup info">You are redirected to the <span className="ph uicontrol">Project Management</span> page of that Organization.</div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Select <span className="ph uicontrol">Invite Members</span> in the upper right corner.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={700} src={useBaseUrl("/d0e77240-9ef1-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/KT-_Invite_users_to_Project.png")} alt="Project Management page" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">The <span className="ph uicontrol">Invite Members</span> dialog appears.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={500} src={useBaseUrl("/df17f420-9ef1-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/KT-_Invite_members_dialog.png")} alt="Invite members dialog" /></div><ol type="a" className="ol substeps"><li className="li substep"><span className="ph cmd">Enter your users' email or group name that you want to invite, then assign a role and project(s) to users.</span></li></ol></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Click <span className="ph uicontrol">Invite</span> to confirm.</span></li></ol> 
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section result">You have added your user(s) to your Project.</section>
