## <a id="task-8231" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Transfer ownership to an existing member

<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section prereq p"><p className="p">You have invited the user to join your Organization. See: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/administration-tasks/manage-users#invite-users-to-join-an-organization">Invite users to join an Organization</a>.</p></div>
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context"><div className="note note note_note"><span className="note__title">Note:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li">Transferring Organization ownership does not affect Project ownership. The previous Organization Owner still has Owner permissions for Projects they created.</li></ul></div><p className="p">Follow these steps to transfer ownership to an existing member:</p></section> 
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Sign in to <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://my.katalon.com/" target="_blank">TestOps Admin</a> and choose your Account.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">On the Account admin page, select your Organization.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={800} src={useBaseUrl("/46cec290-bfe7-11ee-ac6d-0242c7a41fd4/KT-Acount-page.png")} alt="Account admin page" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Once you're on the Organization admin page, select the <span className="ph uicontrol">Users</span> section to open <span className="ph uicontrol">User Management</span> page. You can see a list of active members in the <span className="ph uicontrol">Active Users</span> section.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Navigate to the member you want to select, then click on the <em className="ph i">three dots</em> icon at the end of the row and click <span className="ph uicontrol">Transfer Owner Role</span>.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={800} src={useBaseUrl("/468cb080-bfe7-11ee-ac6d-0242c7a41fd4/KT-Transfer_ownership.png")} alt="User Management page" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">The <span className="ph uicontrol">Transfer Owner Role</span> dialog pops up as below. Double check the member's email address then enter your account's password (if you are using a Katalon account) for authentication.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={500} src={useBaseUrl("/3ab8ea10-76fb-11ee-8403-0242c7a41fd4/KT-Transfer_ownership_for_Google_account.png")} alt="Transfer owner role dialog" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Click on the red <span className="ph uicontrol">Transfer Owner Role</span> button to confirm.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><div className="note warning note_warning"><span className="note__title">Warning:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">You cannot undo this action.</p></li></ul></div></div></li></ol> 
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section result"><p className="p">You have transferred the Organization ownership to the selected email address/member.</p><p className="p">That member is now the new Owner of your Organization. The new Owner has all privileges, including the ability to transfer ownership in the Organization. See: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/administration-roles/administrative-roles-and-permissions">Roles and permissions</a>.</p><p className="p">After transferring ownership, your role automatically becomes the <strong className="ph b">Admin</strong> role.</p></section> 

## <a id="id_2" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Transfer ownership to a new email address

<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">As an Owner of an Organization, the email address you have registered with <span className="ph">Katalon TestOps</span> is bound to your <span className="ph">TestOps</span> Organization and becomes a unique ID. You cannot change this unique ID.</p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">If you want to change your email address, follow these steps:</p> 
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol"><li className="li">Create a new account using your new email address.</li><li className="li">Send an invitation to join the Organization to the new email address. See: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/administration-tasks/manage-users#invite-users-to-join-an-organization">Invite users to join an Organization</a>.</li><li className="li">Join the Organization as a new user. See: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/administration-tasks/join-an-organization">Join TestOps Organizations</a>.</li><li className="li">Transfer ownership to the new email address. See: <a className="xref" href="#">Transfer Ownership</a>.</li><li className="li">Delete the old account. See: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/administration-tasks/manage-users/manage-users#task-2948">Remove existing users</a>.</li></ol>
