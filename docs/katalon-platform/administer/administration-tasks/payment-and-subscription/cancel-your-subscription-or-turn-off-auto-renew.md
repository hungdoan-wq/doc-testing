<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context"><p className="p">The following guide shows you how to cancel your renewal subscription for <span className="ph">Katalon TestOps</span> plan, <span className="ph">TestCloud</span>, <span className="ph">Katalon Studio Enterprise (KSE)</span>, and <span className="ph">Katalon Runtime Engine (KRE)</span>.</p><div className="note important note_important"><span className="note__title">Important:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">You must be the Owner or Billing Manager of your Account.</p></li></ul></div><p className="p">Cancel your subscription or turn off auto-renewal at any time. </p><p className="p">Once the cancellation is successful, you will not be charged for your next billing cycle. </p><p className="p">Subscription cancellation does not issue a refund and the subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.</p><p className="p">Follow these steps to cancel the renewal subscription for your desired product.</p></section>

<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section result"><p className="p">You have canceled the renewal subscription successfully. You can check the Expiry Date in the <span className="ph uicontrol">Your Current Subscription(s)</span> section.</p><p className="p">You can also reactivate your subscription if it has not reached the expiry date yet.</p></section>
