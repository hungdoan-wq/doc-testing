<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="shortdesc">The following is a guide on how to increase your test result quota, your TestCloud sessions as well as KSE/KRE licenses, or switch from a monthly to an annual billing cycle.</p> 
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="note important note_important"><span className="note__title">Important:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">You must be an Owner or Billing Manager of your Account to upgrade a subscription. Refer to the following topic for more information: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/administration-roles/administrative-roles-and-permissions#concept-8930">Roles and permissions at Account and Organization level</a>.</p></li></ul></div>
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">You can upgrade your subscription at any time. Upgrading your subscription does not require you to cancel your existing subscription. </p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">Your account will be upgraded immediately upon successful payment of the prorated amount.</p> 

## <a id="task-gnsprsym" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Upgrade test result quota

<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context"><p className="p"><span className="ph">Katalon TestOps</span> comprises of different modules and services with their own individual quotas. These quotas represent how much of resources you can use according to your subscription plan. Refer to the following topic for more information: <a className="xref" href="#">Version 8.0.0 - 8.0.1</a>.</p><p className="p">Depending on your current <span className="ph">Katalon TestOps</span> subscription plan, there is a limit on the number of test result quotas. To extend the quota, you need to upgrade to a higher tier to extend the test result quota.</p><p className="p">Follow these steps to upgrade test result quota.</p></section> 
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Sign in to <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://my.katalon.com/" target="_blank">TestOps Admin</a> and choose your Account.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">In your Account admin page, select <span className="ph uicontrol">Subscriptions</span> section to open <span className="ph uicontrol">Subscription Management</span> page.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Navigate to <span className="ph uicontrol">Get more single products</span> section.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">In the <span className="ph uicontrol">Select Platform Tier</span> dropdown menu, select a higher tier to increase test result quota.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={700} src={useBaseUrl("/4682ec80-bfe7-11ee-ac6d-0242c7a41fd4/KT-Select_tier.png")} alt="Select Platform Tier" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Click <span className="ph uicontrol">Check out</span>. You are redirected to the checkout page.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">In the checkout page, update the required information for billing and payment method (if needed), then click <span className="ph uicontrol">Checkout</span> to complete your payment.</span></li></ol> 

## <a id="task-vfd5jq4p" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Purchase more KSE/KRE licenses and TestCloud sessions

<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context"><p className="p">Depending on your work requirements, you can:</p><ul className="ul"><li className="li">Purchase more <a className="xref" href="/katalon-studio/katalon-studio-enterprise-and-katalon-runtime-engine-license/katalon-studio-enterprise-per-user-license">Katalon Studio Enterprise per-user license</a> for all users to avoid mixed <span className="ph">Katalon Studio</span> and KSE licenses within an Organization.</li><li className="li">Purchase more <a className="xref" href="/katalon-studio/katalon-studio-enterprise-and-katalon-runtime-engine-license/katalon-runtime-engine-floating-license">Katalon Runtime Engine floating license</a> to run more execution sessions on the same machine.</li><li className="li">Purchase more <span className="ph">TestCloud</span> sessions to increase the number of parallel test executions. To learn more about <span className="ph">TestCloud</span> sessions, see: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/katalon-platform-packages/katalon-platform-quotas#concept-8991">Quotas overview</a>.</li></ul><p className="p">Follow these step to add more KSE/KRE licenses and TestCloud sessions to your subscription.</p></section> 
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Sign in to <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://my.katalon.com/" target="_blank">TestOps Admin</a> and select the desired Account.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">In your Account admin page, select <span className="ph uicontrol">Subscriptions</span> section to open <span className="ph uicontrol">Subscription Management</span> page.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">In the <span className="ph uicontrol">Get more single products</span> section, fill in the total number of licenses or <span className="ph">TestCloud</span> sessions you want to purchase more.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={750} src={useBaseUrl("/473832c0-bfe7-11ee-ac6d-0242c7a41fd4/KT-_Purchase_more.png")} alt="Get more single products section" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Click <span className="ph uicontrol">Checkout</span>. You are redirected to the checkout page.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Fill in or update the required information for billing and payment method (if needed), then click <span className="ph uicontrol">Checkout</span> to complete your purchase.</span></li></ol> 
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section result">After the payment is successfully, the <span className="ph uicontrol">Your Current Subscription(s)</span> section will be updated with the new number of licenses/sessions.</section> 

## <a id="task-xgmrrdhd" class="anchor_top_offset"/>Upgrade to an annual billing cycle

<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context"><div className="note warning note_warning"><span className="note__title">Warning:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">You cannot change your annual subscription back to a monthly           one.</p></li><li className="li"><p className="p">You can only subscribe monthly again once your annual           subscription has ended.</p></li></ul></div><p className="p">Follow these steps to upgrade your subscription to an annual billing cycle.</p></section> 
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Sign in to <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://my.katalon.com/" target="_blank">TestOps Admin</a>.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">In  your Account admin page, select <span className="ph uicontrol">Subscriptions</span> section to open <span className="ph uicontrol">Subscription Management</span> page.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">In the <span className="ph uicontrol">Get more single products</span> section, navigate to your monthly subscription product that you want to upgrade, then switch the billing cycle option  to <span className="ph uicontrol">Annual</span>.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={800} src={useBaseUrl("/46e66940-bfe7-11ee-ac6d-0242c7a41fd4/KT-Billing_plan.png")} alt="Change your billing plan" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Review the new plan's price found under the toggle, then click <span className="ph uicontrol">Check out</span>. You are redirected to the checkout page.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Update the required information for billing and payment method (if needed), then click of <span className="ph uicontrol">Checkout</span> to complete the payment.</span></li></ol> 
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section result">Successful payment will immediately change your subscription   type.</section>
