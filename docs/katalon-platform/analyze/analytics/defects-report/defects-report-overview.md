<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">A software defect is any deviation from the intended behavior or functionality of a program. Identifying and resolving defects promptly is crucial for ensuring software quality and preventing downstream issues. </p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">The Defects Report feature streamlines this process by providing defect traceability as well as actionable insights. Additionally, comprehensive metrics such as a defects' assignee, status, priority, and other metadata are also available at a glance.</p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">This makes it easy to manage software defects, which in turn promotes higher quality output and quicker development cycles. </p>
