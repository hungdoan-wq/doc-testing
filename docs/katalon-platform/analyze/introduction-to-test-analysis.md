---
title: Introduction to test analysis
---


<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">In order to obtain relevant metrics on the progress of your tests as well as the completeness of your requirements, <span className="ph">Katalon TestOps</span> provides test analysis features in both <span className="ph">Katalon Studio</span> and <span className="ph">Katalon TestOps</span>. </p> 
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">These test analysis features makes it easier for your project team to have a clear view of the overall readiness of the project:</p> 
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p"><ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p"><span className="ph">Katalon Studio</span> <a className="xref" href="/katalon-studio/test-reports/view-test-reports/view-and-customize-execution-log-in-katalon-studio#id_1">execution logs</a> and <a className="xref" href="/katalon-studio/test-reports/view-test-reports/view-test-suite-and-test-suite-collection-reports-in-katalon-studio">test suite/test suite collection reports</a> give you an instant insight into how stable your test scripts are. They enable you to locate the root causes of any issues and troubleshoot them quickly.</p></li><li className="li"><p className="p"><span className="ph">Katalon TestOps</span> <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/analyze/reports/view-test-reports/view-testops-dashboard/testops-dashboard-overview">dashboards</a> and <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/analyze/reports/view-test-reports/test-runs-reports-overview">reports</a> allow you to centralize your test data in one place. As a QA manager, you can oversee your team productivity and the status of product quality by looking at release readiness, requirement coverage, traceability matrix, and many more. </p></li></ul></div>
<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">You can also conduct AI image comparison using visual testing in <span className="ph">Katalon TestOps</span>. </p> 
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="p">Refer to the following topics for more information about test analysis features:<ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p"><a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/analyze/reports-and-analytics-overview">Reports overview</a></p></li><li className="li"><p className="p"><a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/analyze/analytics/analytics-overview">Analytics overview</a></p></li></ul></div>
