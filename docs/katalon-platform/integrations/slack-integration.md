<p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="shortdesc">The Slack integration with Katalon TestOps lets users receive notifications directly in a specified Slack channel regarding information on their test results or other related actions performed in TestOps. This enhances project visibility for all members involved and provides a quicker method for checking on work without having to manually navigate to the test result. </p> 
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section prereq p"><ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">You must be the Owner or Administrator of your Katalon project. See: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/administration-roles/administrative-roles-and-permissions">Administrative Roles and Permissions</a>.</p></li><li className="li"><p className="p">You must have permission to install apps onto your Slack workspace. <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://slack.com/help/articles/202035138-Add-apps-to-your-Slack-workspace" target="_blank">Learn more about adding apps to Slack here</a>. </p></li></ul></div>
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section context">TestOps can notify you of your test results' information through a dedicated Slack channel. To set it up, follow these steps: </section> 
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="li stepsection">Set up Incoming Webhooks on Slack</div>
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd"><a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://api.slack.com/messaging/webhooks" target="_blank">Go to this link</a> and click <span className="ph uicontrol">Create your Slack app</span>. </span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Select your preferred configuration, then name your app and choose your workspace.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={500} src={useBaseUrl("/f1a60980-97fb-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/Slack_App_Info_12_11_2023.png")} alt="Name your app and workspace in Slack." /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Click <span className="ph uicontrol">Create App</span>.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Select <span className="ph uicontrol">Incoming Webhooks</span>.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={700} src={useBaseUrl("/26321720-98ba-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/Slack_incoming_webhooks_button_12_12_2023.png")} alt="The incoming webhooks button when setting up Slack apps" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Activate incoming webhooks by clicking on the toggle button. It should be green and say <span className="ph uicontrol">On</span>.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Click on <span className="ph uicontrol">Add New Webhook to Workspace</span>. Grant permission and select the channel you would like to post to. </span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={500} src={useBaseUrl("/f1da8700-97fb-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/Slack_requesting_permission_12_11_2023.png")} alt="Webhook requesting permission to access selected workspace" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Click <span className="ph uicontrol">Allow</span>.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Click <span className="ph uicontrol">Copy</span> to save the Webhook URL to your clipboard. </span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={500} src={useBaseUrl("/ea3df544-ad5e-44a7-9634-5e5664f976da/Slack_Webhook_URL_Censored_12_11_2023.png")} alt="Copy the Webhook URL from settings." /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Save the URL. This is what you will use to set up the Slack integration on TestOps. </span></li></ol> 
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="li stepsection">Integrate Slack with Katalon TestOps</div>
<ol xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="ol steps" start={10}><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Sign in to Katalon TestOps then go to <span className="ph menucascade"><span className="ph uicontrol">Settings</span><abbr title="and then"> &gt; </abbr><span className="ph uicontrol">Integrations</span></span>.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Select Slack by going to <span className="ph menucascade"><span className="ph uicontrol">Collaboration</span><abbr title="and then"> &gt; </abbr><span className="ph uicontrol">Slack</span></span> or by searching it using the search bar. </span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Paste the URL you have copied earlier into the <span className="ph uicontrol">Incoming Webhook URL</span> section, then click <span className="ph uicontrol">Test Connection</span>.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={500} src={useBaseUrl("/5d8c92a0-98aa-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/TO_Integration_with_Slack_URL.png")} alt="Incoming webhook url section in Katalon TestOps" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">A notification informs you of a successful connection. </span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={500} src={useBaseUrl("/f1f4c5c0-97fb-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/TO_connect_susscessfully_Slack.png")} alt="Test connection notification success" /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Click <span className="ph uicontrol">Save</span>. </span></li></ol> 
<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section result">You have integrated Slack with Katalon TestOps. A notification confirms this. <img className="image" width={500} src={useBaseUrl("/f1baa2f0-97fb-11ee-b8c3-0242c7a41fd4/TO_setting_successfully.png")} alt="Slack integration's notifications saved." /></section>
