<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="bodydiv troubleSolution"><section className="section cause"><p className="p">When you receive this message on the login page, this means that your Katalon account has been registered but not yet verified. </p><div className="p">When you register for a new account with your email, Katalon automatically sends a verification email to the email address you used to register your account. If you also try to log in to <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://testops.katalon.io/" target="_blank">Katalon TestOps</a> as an unverified user, a verification email is automatically sent to your email address on file.<div className="note note note_note"><span className="note__title">Note:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">If a Project Owner or Project Admin invites you to a Katalon project, your email is automatically verified if you set up your account from the link in the invitation email.</p></li></ul></div>If you did not receive a verification email, here are some of the reasons why: </div><ol className="ol"><li className="li"><p className="p"><strong className="ph b">Email sent to spam or junk folder</strong>: Your ISP or corporate domain may be configured to deliver commercial mail to this folder by default. Check your spam or junk folder. </p></li><li className="li"><p className="p"><strong className="ph b">Email address is blocked</strong>: Your ISP or corporate domain may have blocked our emails. If you haven't received emails from Katalon, contact your organization's administrator to unblock Katalon emails. If this is not possible, get help from Customer Support (<a className="xref j-external-link" href="mailto:support@katalon.com" target="_blank">support@katalon.com</a>). If all else fails, you may need to register a new Katalon account with an alternative email address.</p></li><li className="li"><div className="p"><strong className="ph b">You registered using a Role, Group, or Alias email address</strong>: Some role addresses can be verified, but many can't, because more than one person may have access to a role email address (ex. <code className="ph codeph">admin@abc.com</code>, <code className="ph codeph">sales@abc.com</code>, <code className="ph codeph">finance@testemail.com</code>). Register using a personal email address or work email address dedicated to your specific use or role (ex. <code className="ph codeph">johnsmith@abc.com</code>) for your contact and billing address.<div className="note note note_note"><span className="note__title">Note:</span> <ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">If your Project Owner or Project Admin invited you on your behalf, ask the Project Owner or Project Manager to invite you again using a personal email address or work email address dedicated to your specific use or role. See: <a className="xref" href="/katalon-platform/administer/administration-tasks/manage-users/manage-users">Manage users</a>.</p></li></ul></div></div></li><li className="li"><p className="p">Check the spelling of your email address. If the spelling is incorrect, sign up again using the correct spelling of the address. If all else fails, you may need to register a new Katalon account with an alternative email address or get help from Customer Support (<a className="xref j-external-link" href="mailto:support@katalon.com" target="_blank">support@katalon.com</a>).</p></li></ol><p className="p">Verify your account by following the steps below: </p></section><section className="section remedy"><ol className="ol steps"><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Go to <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://katalon.com/" target="_blank">katalon.com </a>and log in using your registered email address and password.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><img className="image" width={700} src={useBaseUrl("/6dfdf830-ced5-11ed-a4d3-0242cfbc79b5/troubleshooting-599-step-1.png")} alt="The Katalon website homepage." /></div></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">You will see the unverified account message on the sign-in screen. Katalon automatically sends a verification email to your registered email address.</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Go to your email service provider and open the verification email from Katalon (Subject: "Verify your Katalon account").</span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Click <span className="ph uicontrol">Verify my account</span>. <img className="image" width={700} src={useBaseUrl("/95b64a40-2c58-11ee-bd4d-0242c7a41fd4/Katalon_verify_my_account_email.png")} alt="Verify my account email." /> </span></li><li className="li step stepexpand"><span className="ph cmd">Once your account is verified, you can now log in to <span className="ph">Katalon Studio</span> and your account is no longer blocked.</span><div className="itemgroup info"><div className="note note note_note"><span className="note__title">Note:</span> <p className="p">If your account is still blocked upon logging in or if the verification prompt did not continue, wait for 5 minutes, refresh your browser and try again by clicking <span className="ph uicontrol">Resend email</span>.</p></div></div></li></ol></section></div>
