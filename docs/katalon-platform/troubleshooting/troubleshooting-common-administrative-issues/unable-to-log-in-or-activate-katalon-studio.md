---
title: Unable to log in or activate Katalon Studio
---


<section xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="section condition"><p className="p">There are a few possible reasons why you are unable to log in or activate Katalon Studio. Refer to the following reasons and solutions below. </p></section> 
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="bodydiv troubleSolution"><section className="section cause"><div className="p">When you log in to Katalon Studio and encounter the following message: <pre className="pre codeblock"><code>Mixing paid products and free Studio within an account is not permitted in our Terms of Use. Contact your Katalon Admin/Account Owner if you need to purchase a license.</code></pre></div><p className="p">This happens when you are a Katalon Studio free user who belongs to an Account with paid Katalon products, which is not permitted due to the <strong className="ph b">Free Offerings</strong> term in <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://katalon.com/terms#customer-terms-of-use" target="_blank">Terms of Use</a>. You then will be blocked from using free Katalon Studio. </p></section><section className="section remedy"><div className="li step p"><span className="ph cmd">Try one of the following solutions to resolve the issue:</span><div className="itemgroup info"><ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">If you need to continue to use Katalon Studio, contact your Organization Owner/Admin to assign you a paid KSE license.</p></li><li className="li"><p className="p">If you no longer need to use Katalon Studio, you can stop using the free Studio license.</p></li></ul><p className="p">Contact our Customer Success Team at <a className="xref j-external-link" href="mailto:success@katalon.com" target="_blank">success@katalon.com</a> for further assistance.</p></div></div></section></div>
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="bodydiv troubleSolution"><section className="section cause"><div className="p">When you log in to Katalon Studio and encounter the following message:<pre className="pre codeblock"><code>Your trial has expired. Please ask your Administrator to assign a Katalon Studio Enterprise license or download the free package of Katalon Studio to continue.</code></pre></div><p className="p">This happens when your trial plan has expired but you are still using the <span className="ph">Katalon Studio Enterprise</span> edition.</p></section><section className="section remedy"><div className="li step p"><span className="ph cmd">Try one of the following solutions to resolve the issue: </span><div className="itemgroup info"><ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">Purchase a paid KSE license to continue using advanced features. </p></li><li className="li"><p className="p">Download and install the <span className="ph">Katalon Studio</span> on the <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://katalon.com/download" target="_blank">Katalon download page</a> if you want to use Katalon Studio free version.</p></li></ul></div></div></section></div>
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="bodydiv troubleSolution"><section className="section cause"><div className="p">When you activate Katalon Studio and encounter the following message: <pre className="pre codeblock"><code>You are using a legacy version of Katalon Studio. Please upgrade to the latest version to continue.{"\n"}</code></pre></div><p className="p">You are using the old version of Katalon Studio.</p></section><section className="section remedy"><div className="li step p"><span className="ph cmd">Download and install the latest version on <a className="xref j-external-link" href="https://katalon.com/download" target="_blank">Katalon download page</a>. There are two types of the latest version:</span><div className="itemgroup info"><ul className="ul"><li className="li"><p className="p">The <span className="ph">Katalon Studio</span> edition is for free users.</p></li><li className="li"><p className="p">The <span className="ph">Katalon Studio Enterprise</span> edition is for users who have a trial plan or a paid KSE license.</p></li></ul></div></div></section></div>
<div xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml" className="bodydiv troubleSolution"><section className="section cause"><p className="p">From Katalon Studio version 9.3.0 onwards, we provide a new login SAML approach. Upon clicking <span className="ph uicontrol">Log in from browsers</span>, you are directed to a new URL with the local server <code className="ph codeph">127.0.0.1</code>. If you're in a highly secure environment, you might encounter issues with logging in.</p></section><section className="section remedy"><div className="li step p"><span className="ph cmd"> To enable access, you need to request the IT team to whitelist port <code className="ph codeph">127.0.0.1</code> to your proxy settings.</span></div></section></div>
